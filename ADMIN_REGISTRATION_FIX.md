### Step 2: Go to Registration Page
1. Open browser: http://localhost:3000
2. Click "Create a new account"

### Step 3: Fill Registration Form
- **Name**: Your full name
- **Email**: Your email address
- **Password**: At least 4 characters (must include uppercase, lowercase, and number)
  - Example: `Admin123`
- **Account Type**: Select "Administrator"
- **Department**: Optional (can leave blank)
- **Company**: Optional (can leave blank)

### Step 4: Create Account
Click "Create account" - your admin account will be created!
